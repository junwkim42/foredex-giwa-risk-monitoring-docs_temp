# ForeDex Capabilities and Future Expansion

## ForeDex

ForeDex is a blockchain-based on-chain data platform. It develops data infrastructure for digital-asset and tokenized-asset markets through AI-based address classification and on-chain data analysis.

## ForeDex Capabilities

- Experience collecting and normalizing on-chain data and associating it at the project level
- An analytical structure that reviews project-related addresses, token supply, and DEX activity together
- An evidence-oriented verification workflow from dashboard results to the public ledger
- A data model designed to extend address, rule, and asset-type coverage as the number of projects grows

## Related Intellectual Property

ForeDex holds Korean Patent No. 10-2987801, `스테이블코인 디페깅 사전 탐지 시스템` (`Stablecoin Depegging Early Detection System`), relating to risk-detection technology.

## Potential Expansion within the GIWA Ecosystem

1. Expand coverage of core wallets and operating addresses for each project.
2. Add detection rules tailored to tokens, RWAs, stablecoins, and DeFi protocols.
3. Enrich transaction context through address labels and entity identification.
4. Enhance rule-specific evidence transactions and the operator review workflow.
5. Evaluate alert and project-information modules that could be used at user touchpoints such as GIWA Wallet.
